Immigration law

Immigration is the process through which individuals become permanent residents or citizens of another country . The guiding force of Nigeria Immigration is principally from statutes and also supported by policy. These laws and Policies governs the administration of immigration laws and visa in Nigeria . The Fundamental law is the IMMIGRATION ACT of 2015 Which is supported by the Immigration Regulations of 2017 and also the new visa Policy which was launched by President Muhammed buhari in 2020 which introduced a reformed visa regime in Nigeria.
