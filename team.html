KEHINDE OLAOLU SOREMEKUN LLB (HONS), BL, NOTARY PUBLIC,

PRINCIPAL AND MANAGING PARTNER

Kehinde Soremekun was called to the Nigerian Bar on the 7th day of November, 2006 and has been in active legal practice since becoming a lawyer.
