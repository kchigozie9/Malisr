IMMIGRATION LAW


Immigration Law is the process through which individuals become permanent residents or citizens of another country . The guiding force of Nigeria Immigration is principally from statutes and also supported by policy. These laws and Policies governs the administration of immigration laws and visa in Nigeria . The Fundamental law is the IMMIGRATION ACT of 2015 Which is supported by the Immigration Regulations of 2017 and also the new visa Policy which was launched by President Muhammed buhari in 2020 which introduced a reformed visa regime in Nigeria..

In MALISR SOLICITORS, we have taken our time to study and digest this Laws to help suit the needs of our clients.

We also assist in the Applying for expartriate Quota .

Expatriate Quota in Nigeria is an approval granted by the Minister of Interior affairs to foreigners to register companies to enable foreign employees,directors or owners of business work in Nigeria.

A business Permit is often processed with an expatriate and a company that wishes to employ a foreigner is expected to obtain the Expatriate quota approval before a temporary work permit.
We assist our clients in procuring all of the above and also more in so far as it relates to Immigration Law.
